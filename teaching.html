Teaching Approach

My teaching approach combines socio-interactionism and cognitivism, supplemented with behaviorist elements for foundational practice. I believe in:

  • Active participation through discussions and problem-solving.
  • Real-world applications to make math more meaningful.
  • Scaffolding and support for non-native English speakers learning mathematics and physics.
